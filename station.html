Easy Japanese: At the Station

Essential phrases for navigating Japanese trains and stations.

Listen to the pronunciation

Example Dialogue

Traveler すみません。駅はどこですか？ Sumimasen. Eki wa doko desu ka? Excuse me. Where is the station?
Staff あそこです。 Asoko desu. It is over there.
Traveler 新宿までいくらですか？ Shinjuku made ikura desu ka? How much is it to Shinjuku?
Staff 200円です。 Ni-hyaku en desu. It is 200 yen.
