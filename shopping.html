Easy Japanese: Shopping (Kaimono)

Master the essential phrases for buying things in Japan.

Listen to the pronunciation

Example Dialogue

Customer すみません。これはいくらですか？ Sumimasen. Kore wa ikura desu ka? Excuse me. How much is this?
Staff それは 1,000円です。 Sore wa sen-en desu. That is 1,000 yen.
Customer じゃあ、これをください。 Jaa, kore o kudasai. Then, I'll take this, please.

いくら (How much) ください (Please) これ (This)

Grammar Explanation

1. 〜はいくらですか？ (wa ikura desu ka?)

The standard way to ask for a price. Just put the item name before "wa".

2. 〜をください (o kudasai)

Used when ordering or buying something. "Kudasai" means "Please give me".
