Easy Japanese: At a Restaurant

Learn how to order your favorite food in Japanese.

Listen to the pronunciation

Example Dialogue

Customer すみません。メニューをお願いします。 Sumimasen. Menyuu o onegaishimasu. Excuse me. Menu, please.
Staff はい、どうぞ。 Hai, douzo. Yes, here you go.
Customer （指をさして）これをお願いします。 (Yubi o sashite) Kore o onegaishimasu. (Pointing) I'll have this, please.

Check Vocabulary

メニュー (Menu) お願いします (Please) 注文 (Order)

Grammar Explanation

1. 〜をお願いします (o onegaishimasu)

A polite way to request something. It's more formal than "kudasai".

2. どうぞ (douzo)

Used when offering something to someone. "Here you are."
